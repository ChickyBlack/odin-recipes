Caesar Salad

Description

Classic Caesar Salad with crisp homemade croutons and a light caesar dressing, for when you want to impress your dinner guests.

Ingredients

  • Lettuce
  • Croutons
  • Salad Dressing
  • Bacon bits
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Wash and chop lettuce
  2. Spin/dry lettuce and place in bowl
  3. Add croutons
  4. Add bacon bits
  5. Add cheese
  6. Add salad dressing
  7. Toss and enjoy!
